Mathieu Aubry
He/Him - They/Them

I am a senior researcher at École des Ponts ParisTech (ENPC) in the Imagine group of the A3SI team, LIGM lab (UMR 8049), and an Ellis scholar attached to the Paris Unit.

I am interested in computer vision with a focus on unsupervised image collection analysis ( ERC DISCOVER ), and applications to historical data (also ANR VHS and ANR EIDA ) and Earth imagery analysis. To get an overview of my research, you can look at the ANR EnHerit (2017-2023) and ERC DISCOVER websites.

If you are interested in working with me, fill this form (I will not answer e-mail applications).
